About Us

Who We Are

We are the local Canton, OH chapter of Food Not Bombs, a nationwide mutual aid organization. Our chapter is dedicated to providing mutual aid to our community members and neighbors in the form of sharing food and other necessities weekly.


Each chapter operates somewhat differently. Our chapter provides a hot meal to community members every Saturday, rain or shine, no strings attached.

Our Mission & Values

We believe believes food is a human right. We believe that governments fighting wars while allowing their citizens to live in poverty is a form of violence. We believe that when we share food, we are starting a revolution.

What To Expect at a Share

  • A free vegetarian meal, no questions asked, no ID or religious affiliation required.
  • Toiletries and seasonal needs (i.e. hats, sunscreen, hand sanitizer).
  • Harm reduction supplies such as Narcan and condoms.
  • Reading materials, including informational pamphlets and zines. We put our zines out quarterly!
