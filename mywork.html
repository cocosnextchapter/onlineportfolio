Branding & Illustration

For this design problem, I was asked to create a brand identity for a fictional mobile coffee cart called Perk Up. The brand’s mission is "to make mornings more joyful through bright design and quick service."

I chose a combination of a sunflower, a rising sun, and a coffee bean to represent the brand's role in a customer's morning "pick-me-up" routine. I chose a sunflower due to their bright coloring and penchant for facing towards sunlight and brightness.

For this icon, I was tasked with creating a simplified icon to represent a fandom that I am a part of. I chose crochet. I didn't want to just represent crochet, but the common experience that many fiber artist contend with: carpal tunnel.

This led to my creation of "Pins and Needles", my iconic representation of the malady. I chose warm reds for my color choices to represent the pain that accompanies carpal tunnel.
